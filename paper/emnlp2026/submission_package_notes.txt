ARR / EMNLP 2026 submission-readiness checklist:

1. Authorship and anonymity
- Confirm the final ARR author list before submission; EMNLP commitment will not allow adding or removing authors later.
- Check the PDF title page, metadata, acknowledgments, appendix, and notes for any institution, personal email, homepage, repo, or cloud-link leakage.
- If code or assets are shared during review, ensure they are anonymized and do not reveal lab identity.

2. Venue and formatting
- Confirm that the submission uses official ACL style in anonymous review mode.
- Re-check main-paper length against ARR long-paper expectations (8 pages of main content, with references excluded under ARR rules as applicable).
- Decide whether the current appendix should stay appended in the review PDF or be moved into supplementary material, depending on the exact ARR submission interface and page-policy interpretation used at submission time.
- Decide explicitly whether the upload should use `insight_emnlp.pdf`, the
  strict candidate `insight_emnlp_arr8.pdf`, or the current oral-readthrough
  candidate `insight_emnlp_oral8.pdf`; do not use
  `insight_emnlp_polished.pdf` for upload.

3. Scientific scope and claims
- Keep the paper framed as a language-grounded semantic interface / ontology-as-modeling paper, not as a pure leaderboard paper.
- Keep the strongest claims on semantic-interface evidence, ontology construction, and beyond-accuracy evaluation.
- Keep the current caution that actor-based timing evidence is partial and dataset-dependent.

4. References and related work
- Manually inspect the bibliography for missing entries, malformed fields, duplicated items, or venue strings that look inconsistent.
- Do one final human pass on very recent related work most relevant to EMNLP positioning: multimodal semantics, concept bottlenecks, ontology construction, language-mediated supervision, and interpretable decision making.

5. Required policy-facing sections
- Confirm that limitations remain explicit and visible.
- Confirm that no citation or factual claim is hallucinated or unsupported.
- Confirm that any LLM-assisted ontology construction is described as part of the method, not hidden or overstated.
- Confirm that writing-assistance usage is compliant with ACL/ARR policy.

6. ARR workflow logistics
- Submit to ARR by the target cycle deadline.
- Ensure every author completes the required ARR reviewer registration workflow by the stated deadline.
- Record the intended preferred venue as EMNLP 2026 during ARR submission.
- Prepare for author response and discussion window after reviews are released.

7. Final package sanity checks before upload
- Open the compiled PDF and visually inspect the first page, table rendering, figure captions, line breaks, and appendix start.
- Check that all key tables and figures referenced in the main text actually appear and are legible.
- Confirm that the latest insight_emnlp.pdf and insight_emnlp_first25.pdf match the latest source edits.
- Keep a frozen copy of the exact submitted PDF and source package for later rebuttal / revision tracking.
